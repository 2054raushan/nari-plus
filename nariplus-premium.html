Our Story

Built around one belief: comfort is a right, not a luxury.

Nari Plus began with a simple frustration — premium hygiene care felt out of reach for most Indian women. We set out to change that, engineering a pad that matches international standards of comfort and protection, without the international price tag.

Every pack that leaves our facility is tested, checked, and made with one person in mind: you, on your busiest day.
